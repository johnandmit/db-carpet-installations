About DB Carpet Installations

Our Story

Quality Carpet Installation Auckland Trusts

DB Carpet Installations was founded by David with a simple mission: deliver quality carpet installation that Auckland homeowners can trust.

What started as a passion for precision flooring work has grown into one of Auckland's most recommended carpet installation services, earning a perfect 5.0 star rating across 52 reviews.

David and his team take pride in every job, whether it is a full home installation, a single bedroom, or a small repair. The focus has always been on doing the work right, communicating clearly, and charging fairly.
