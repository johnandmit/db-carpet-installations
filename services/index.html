Our Services

Residential

Residential Carpet Installation

Whether you are fitting out a single bedroom or an entire home, we handle every residential carpet installation with precision.

We work with carpets you have sourced yourself or help you find the right fit for your space. Our team measures, cuts, and installs with clean joins and smooth finishes that last.

  • Full home carpet installation
  • Single room fitting (bedrooms, lounges, hallways)
  • Work with your own carpet or help you source it
  • Precision measurements and seamless joins
  • Clean, professional finish every time

Garage

Garage Carpet Installation

Transform your garage into a clean, functional space. Garage carpet adds warmth, protects your floor, and gives your garage a finished look.

We measure and install to fit any garage layout, working around shelving, vehicles, and other fixtures.

  • Custom fitted to any garage size and shape
  • Durable carpet suited for garage use
  • Clean, professional finish
  • Protection for your concrete floor

Repairs

Carpet Repairs

Loose corners, lifted edges, or wear damage? We handle carpet repairs of all sizes. No job is too small.

David personally inspects every repair to make sure it is done right. Most repairs are completed in a single visit.

  • Loose corners and lifted edges re-secured
  • Seam repairs and re-joining
  • Wear and damage patching
  • Quick turnaround, most jobs done in one visit
